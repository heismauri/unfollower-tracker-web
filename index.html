Unfollower Tracker

made to work with Instagram

How to use it

  1. Request your Instagram Data, here is a tutorial on how to do it. IMPORTANT: The requested format should be JSON. Depending on your account this might take some hours.
  2. Download your Instagram Data, and find the folder followers_and_following. Then upload the following.json and followers.json files in the form below.
  3. Click "Find who unfollowed you!" and a list of people will be shown at the end of the page.
